Who We Are

Engineers creating opportunities for future engineers

Mission

An employee owned tech cafe by engineers, for engineers. Training future teachers on-site, free of charge. Ultimately, having enough teachers to give free computer science education to K - 12 students that do not have robust programs in their reach.

Business Model

The profits from the coffee and repair bar fund the education for creating cutting-edge computer science teachers. Employees become employee-owners under an Employee Stock Ownership (ESOP) trust.

Education Costs

Over the years have kept soaring without any other option than more loans. We want to reverse this trend. Education is a multi-year investment. We want to employ engineers in training that need a single place to work and study, especially to have health benefits and provide for their families.

Disruptive Technologies

There are world-wide shortages for data scientists as only 6 of 29 universities offer undergraduate programs, the rest are graduate and Ph.D level. Blockchain experts are in demand as well. Our goal is to get half of each cohort to sign a pledge to go into more specialized technology niches.

Bureaucracy

Computer science graduates make double what computer science teachers make. They are capable of teaching, but are not incentivized. Many employees at tech companies get paid days to attend yearly industry conferences, schools can barely pay teachers.

Conversation Starter

This idea is unconventional because the whole premise is a coffee shop as a community hub starting the conversation on emerging technologies, while recruiting from there.
